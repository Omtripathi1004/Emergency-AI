Interactive First Aid Reference

🩸 Heavy Bleeding
  1. Apply direct pressure with a clean cloth or bandage.
  2. Keep pressure applied until bleeding stops or help arrives.
  3. If limbs are bleeding severely and pressure fails, prepare a tourniquet high and tight.
💧 Water Submersion / Near Drowning
  1. Remove person safely from water. Avoid putting yourself in danger.
  2. Check responsiveness and breathing. If unconscious/not breathing, call 911 immediately.
  3. Initiate CPR (30 chest compressions, followed by 2 rescue breaths).
🦴 Fractures & Breaks
  1. Do not attempt to push bone back or align the limb.
  2. Splint the joint above and below the fracture to stabilize it.
  3. Apply a cold pack wrapped in a cloth to reduce swelling.
